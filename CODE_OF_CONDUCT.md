# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. Math Blast is a space where:
- All are welcome regardless of age, body, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
- Ideas and feedback are valued
- Harassment of any kind is not tolerated

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Insulting/derogatory comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Sharing private information without consent
- Trolling or bad-faith arguments

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [maintainer email]. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly. The project maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Consequences for violations:
1. Verbal warning
2. Temporary restriction
3. Permanent ban

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues and PRs
- Discord/community servers
- Email communications
- Social media discussions
- Project-related events
